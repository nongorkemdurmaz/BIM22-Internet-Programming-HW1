About Us

    Eskişehir Technical University of Eskişehir Technical University, Science, Culture, and also in the center of Eskişehir, which is the city of Eskişehir, which is published in the Official Gazette, which is published in the Official Gazette. Consists of the Application Center.

    Eskişehir Technical University works to prepare its students as the most equipped and competitive individuals for the future in creative and dynamic educational environments, with its competent teaching staff, each of whom follows the developments in their field and shares all their time with their students, in its units with a strong infrastructure, where it carries out education and research activities. The operation of the international Hasan Polatkan Airport, located in the 2 Eylül Campus of our university, is carried out by our Faculty of Aviation and Space Sciences. There are international flights to Hasan Polatkan Airport by different airline companies on various days of the week.
